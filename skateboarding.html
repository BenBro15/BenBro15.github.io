A New Passion I found

This past Summer I decided to invest a little money into buying a decent skateboard since I have enjoyed snowboarding so much. I hadn't really tried skateboarding before this because it was a lot harder than snowboarding, there was a lot more risk of injury and I played other sports in high school that were more important, and the skatepark was somewhat of a sketchy place. However now that sports are done and theres a relatively new skatepark that was built ten minutes from my house my friend and I decided to give it a try. I was extremely surprised at how much I enjoyed it and over the summer I went probably 3-4 times a week. I went from barely being able to ride and hoping nobody was there to feeling comfortable on every feature and knowing everyone who was a regular there by name. This was an unexpected venture but i am very happy I tried something new.
